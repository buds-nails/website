About Me

I'm a certified Gel and BIAB nail technician based in Newton Longville, Milton Keynes. I specialise in natural nails because I believe the best manicures start with nail health.

Whether you're after a classic gel mani, a strengthening BIAB treatment, or some gorgeous nail art — I've got you covered.

Every appointment is relaxed, friendly, and all about you. Pop the kettle on, pick your colour, and let's make your nails look amazing.

Get in Touch

Gel & BIAB Nails in Newton Longville and Across Milton Keynes

I'm based right in the middle of Newton Longville (MK17), which puts me perfectly placed for nails across south-west Milton Keynes and all the surrounding villages. Whether you're popping over from Whaddon, Great Horwood, Mursley, Stewkley, Winslow, Bletchley, Fenny Stratford, Wavendon, Stoke Hammond, Little Brickhill or anywhere else nearby — I'd love to have you in.

Because it's just me doing the nails, every appointment is calm, unhurried and properly personal. No rushed conveyor belt, no loud salon chatter, just a relaxed chat and a lovely set of nails at the end.

I'm open Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm, which works really well if you can sneak out on a lunch break, work flexibly from home, or just prefer a quiet weekday slot over a busy Saturday queue. If weekday hours don't fit — pop me a DM on Instagram and we'll see what we can sort.
